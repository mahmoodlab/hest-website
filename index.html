HEST-1k: A Dataset Integrating Spatial Transcriptomics
and Histology Image Analysis

Designed for biomarker discovery, multimodal representation learning, and benchmarking foundation models for histology.

Dataset Overview

Combining Spatial Transcriptomics and Histology.

  • 1,108 paired samples

  • 25 organs

  • 1.5 million spots with morphology

  • 60 million nuclei
