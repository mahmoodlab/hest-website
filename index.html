HEST-1k: A dataset integrating Histology Image Analysis
and Spatial Transcriptomics

Designed for benchmarking (HEST-Benchmakr), biomarker discovery and multimodal representation learning.

Dataset Overview

Combining Spatial Transcriptomics and Histology.

  • 1,108 paired samples

  • 25 organs

  • 1.5 million spots with morphology

  • 60 million nuclei
